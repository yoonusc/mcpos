MCPos Data Deletion Policy

How to Request Account Deletion:

MCPos does not store any user data on external servers. All data is stored locally on your device.

To delete all data, simply uninstall the app. All locally stored data will be permanently deleted with the app.

Data Collection and Usage:

We do not collect or store any of your personal data, including your location. All data used within the app is solely for functionality and is never shared with third parties.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions, please contact us at anycodeslabs@gmail.com, or go to MCPos Home > More > Support > Contact Us. We will help you.
